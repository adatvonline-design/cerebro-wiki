1. Grace Notes - second Keypad layout. ( Press or click Grace Note ("/" key) then input as normal - return to first Keypad layout to select rhythm, accidentals and articulations as usual. Don't forget to turn off 'grace note' after input - click on the Grace Note key again). 

2. Cue-sized notes (Select some notes then press Enter key on second Keypad layout) 

3. Small notehead (Select some notes and choose the small notehead from the pull-down menu in the 'Notes' section of the Properties Window).
