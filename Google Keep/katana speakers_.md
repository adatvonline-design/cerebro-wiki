I recently picked up the Katana 212 combo and started gigging with it. Finding it to provide extremely enjoyable stage tone. I run the line out directly into the PA board. With the new version of the software, V2, you have some nice control over the EQ and speaker/mic emulation for the line out signal. And of course, V2 also provides a ton more internal EQ options and it also doubles the number of presets. The only thing I didn’t care for was the two built-in Katana branded speakers. They are okay for OEM speakers, but I swapped them out for two of my favorite Celestions which are the G12H90 model
