I've encountered this issue before. I would take a different route. Remove/disable the red outline, so you just have black text. Copy that text node, paste an instance. In the copy, go to the shading tab, select element 1, and deinstance the color group by right clicking either the red, green or blue parameters. Then, change it to red, select the outline option next to "appearance," and merge it under the black text, then increase its thickness to the desired level. Hope that was clear :)

EDIT: should have mentioned you need to open the title in Fusion, and expand the node group to access the source text node
