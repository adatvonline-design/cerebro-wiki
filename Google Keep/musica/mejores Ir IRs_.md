Based on the IR options you have, here's a breakdown of the best options and why:
Tay ESN: This is likely the best option. The "ES-N" (Expression System - Nylon) is a Taylor-designed undersaddle transducer specifically for their nylon-string models. Since your Ovation CC059 is also a nylon-string guitar with an undersaddle piezo, this IR is designed to complement that type of pickup and string material, aiming for a natural acoustic sound.
Here's why other options might be less ideal:
Acoustic Piezo Standard: This is a generic piezo IR. While your guitar has a piezo, "Tay ESN" is likely more refined for nylon strings.
Dean Markley DM3000: Dean Markley makes various pickups, but the DM3000 is often associated with soundhole magnetic pickups for steel-string acoustics. This would not be ideal for your nylon-string piezo.
Fishman AGX / Fishman SBT: Fishman makes many types of acoustic pickups. AGX likely refers to an undersaddle piezo, but without more specifics on the model, it's hard to say if it's optimized for nylon. SBT stands for "Soundboard Transducer," which captures vibrations from the top of the guitar, a different pickup principle than your Ovation's undersaddle piezo.
